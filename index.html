Willow Journal — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

Willow Journal is designed to keep your information private.

What we collect

Nothing

Willow Journal does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, and no third-party tracking.

Where your data lives

Everything you create — folders, notes, checklists, photos and stickers — is stored only on your device. Photos and stickers you add are copied into the app's private storage on your phone. None of it is uploaded to us or anyone else.

If you have iCloud Backup enabled on your iPhone, your device data (including this app's data) may be included in your personal iCloud backup. That backup is controlled entirely by you and Apple under Apple's privacy terms — we have no access to it.

Permissions

Willow Journal asks for Photos and Camera access only so you can add pictures to your notes and folders. Images are used solely inside the app and stay on your device.

Children

The app collects no data and is suitable for all ages.
